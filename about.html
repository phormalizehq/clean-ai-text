About CleanTextApp

Bridging the gap between AI generation and professional communication.

Our Mission

As AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini become part of our daily workflows, we noticed a recurring friction point: copying AI responses often results in messy Markdown artifacts, inconsistent spacing, and symbols that distract from the message.

We built CleanTextApp to empower professionals, students, and creators to instantly "purify" their text. Whether you are preparing an executive email, a school report, or a social media post, our tool ensures your content looks hand-crafted, professional, and polished.

Why CleanTextApp?

Our utility isn't just a simple formatter. We combine text stripping logic with real-time analysis to give you a suite of tools that improve your workflow:

  • Instant Purification: One-click removal of Markdown clutter and special characters.
  • Readability Metrics: Real-time word counts and reading time estimates to help you gauge engagement.
  • Case Manipulation: Quickly toggle between Title Case, UPPERCASE, and lowercase for headlines and lists.
  • Word Frequency: Identify overused words to keep your writing fresh and diverse.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our core principle. CleanTextApp does not store, transmit, or view your text. Our software operates entirely on the "client-side," meaning the processing happens inside your own web browser. Your sensitive data never hits a server, ensuring that your private work remains private.

The Team

CleanTextApp is maintained by a small team of developers and writers who believe that AI should assist human creativity, not clutter it. We are dedicated to keeping this tool fast, free, and accessible to everyone who values clean communication.

For inquiries, feedback, or support, please contact us at support@cleantextapp.com.
