About CleanTextApp

Bridging the gap between AI generation and professional communication.

Our Mission

As AI tools like ChatGPT and Claude become part of our daily lives, we noticed a recurring problem: copying text often results in messy Markdown formatting, strange symbols, and inconsistent spacing.

We built CleanTextApp to help professionals, students, and creators instantly "purify" their text for emails, reports, and social media. Our goal is to make AI-generated content look like it was hand-crafted by you, not a machine.

How It Works

Our tool runs entirely in your web browser. When you paste text, our custom scripts strip away the markdown clutter while preserving the actual structure of your content. We provide real-time readability scores and text manipulation tools (like find-and-replace) to give you total control over the final output.

Privacy First

Your data is your business. CleanTextApp does not store your text. Everything you paste into our editor stays in your browser's local memory and is never sent to a server. When you refresh or close the page, your text is gone‚Äîensuring your sensitive drafts remain private.
