Master the tools to make your AI-generated content professional and clean.

1 Basic Purification

Paste your text from ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini and click Purify Text. Our algorithm automatically:

  • Removes Markdown characters (**, #, `).
  • Cleans up symbols inside words while keeping hyphens and slashes.
  • Fixes sentence casing (capitalizing the first letter of sentences).

2 Find & Replace

Use the toolbar to quickly swap out specific words or phrases. On mobile, these inputs stack vertically to give you more room to type. This is perfect for changing names, dates, or specific terms across your entire document instantly.

3 Case Formatting & Metrics

Quickly convert your entire document to UPPERCASE or lowercase using the one-click buttons in the toolbar. As you work, keep an eye on your Readability Score and Reading Time to ensure your message is clear and concise.

4 Local & Private

Everything you do happens in your browser. When you "Purify," no data is sent to a server. Simply copy your clean text and close the tab‚Äîyour sensitive drafts stay with you.
