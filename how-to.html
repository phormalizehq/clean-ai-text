Master the tools to make your AI-generated content professional and clean.

1. Basic Purification

Paste your text from ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini and click Purify Text. Our algorithm automatically:

  • Removes Markdown characters (**, #, `).
  • Cleans up symbols inside words while keeping hyphens and slashes.
  • Fixes sentence casing (capitalizing the first letter of sentences).

2. Find & Replace

Use the toolbar to quickly swap out specific words or phrases. On mobile, these inputs will stack vertically to give you more room to type.

3. Case Formatting

Quickly convert your entire document to UPPERCASE or lowercase using the one-click buttons in the toolbar.
